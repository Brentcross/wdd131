About Nampa, Idaho

Nampa is a small city in the Treasure Valley of Idaho. It is well known for its agricultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, and a welcoming community. Popular attractions include Lake Lowell, the Warhawk Air Museum, and the Nampa Farmers Market.
